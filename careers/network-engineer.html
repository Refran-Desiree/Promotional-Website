Senior Network Engineer

Join our team in Japan and help build the future of network infrastructure.

Position Overview

We are seeking an experienced Senior Network Engineer to join our team in Japan. This role is crucial in designing, implementing, and maintaining our clients' network infrastructure while ensuring optimal performance and security.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field
  • 5+ years of experience in network engineering
  • CCNP or equivalent certification required
  • Strong knowledge of routing protocols (BGP, OSPF, EIGRP)
  • Experience with network security and firewalls
  • Proficiency in network monitoring tools
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills
  • Fluent in English and Japanese

Responsibilities

  • Design and implement network infrastructure solutions
  • Configure and maintain routers, switches, and firewalls
  • Monitor network performance and troubleshoot issues
  • Implement security measures and best practices
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams
  • Document network configurations and procedures
  • Mentor junior network engineers

Benefits

  • Competitive salary package
  • Health insurance and wellness programs
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Flexible work arrangements
  • Modern office environment
  • International work experience

How to Apply

To apply for this position, please send your resume and cover letter to careers@404solutions.com with the subject line "Senior Network Engineer - Japan".

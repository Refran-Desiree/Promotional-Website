IT Support Specialist

Join our dynamic team in California and help deliver exceptional technical support to our valued clients.

Position Overview

We are seeking a talented IT Support Specialist to join our growing team in California. In this role, you'll be the frontline of our technical support, providing exceptional service and solutions to our clients' IT challenges. You'll work with cutting-edge technologies and be part of a collaborative team that values innovation and customer satisfaction.

Requirements

  • Associate's degree in Information Technology or related field (Bachelor's preferred)
  • 2+ years of experience in IT support or help desk role
  • CompTIA A+ certification required (Network+ preferred)
  • Strong experience with Windows and macOS operating systems
  • Solid understanding of networking fundamentals
  • Experience with ticketing systems and IT service management
  • Excellent customer service and communication skills
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical abilities
  • Ability to work independently and in a team environment

Responsibilities

  • Provide exceptional technical support via phone, email, and in-person
  • Diagnose and resolve hardware, software, and network issues
  • Install, configure, and maintain computer systems and applications
  • Create and maintain detailed documentation of support tickets and solutions
  • Assist with network troubleshooting and maintenance
  • Conduct user training sessions for new technologies
  • Participate in on-call rotation for after-hours support
  • Contribute to IT projects and initiatives
  • Stay current with emerging technologies and best practices

Benefits & Perks

  • Competitive salary with performance bonuses
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance
  • 401(k) matching program
  • Generous paid time off and holidays
  • Professional development and certification support
  • Modern office environment with latest technology
  • Flexible work arrangements
  • Regular team building and social events
  • Career growth opportunities

Ready to Join Our Team?

Take the next step in your IT career with 404: Solutions Found. We're looking for passionate individuals who are excited about technology and helping others.
